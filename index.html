Cash234 – bank branch of the future

At Cash234, we are on a mission to bring banking and payment services to every community in Nigeria – closer than ever before.

Our belief is that; ubiquitous and affordable access to basic banking and payment services (financial inclusion) is critical to uplifting people out of poverty and that is why we are working with retail shops in our local communities to offer banking services closer to where our people live and work.

Join us on this journey by signing up → as Cash234 Agent today.

As Cash234 Agent, you are our lifeblood. You will be trained and equipped to offer simple banking services out of your shop, in your community and earn up to ₦250,000 or more in monthly profit.

Services available on Cash234 include, cash deposit to all banks in Nigeria, cash withdrawal from any bank account in Nigeria, funds transfer, bills payment, airtime top-up, bank account opening, loans, pensions, insurance and many more to come.
